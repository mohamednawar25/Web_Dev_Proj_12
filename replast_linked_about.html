Our Story

Founded by a team of passionate students at AASTMIT, Alexandria, RePlast Creations was born from the desire to make a real environmental impact. By recycling plastic waste, we give new life to materials that would otherwise harm our planet.

Our Mission

To create durable, affordable, and sustainable furniture while raising awareness about plastic pollution and environmental responsibility. We believe that every piece of recycled plastic furniture is a step towards a cleaner, greener Egypt.

Our Vision

To become Egypt's leading eco-friendly furniture manufacturer, inspiring communities to embrace sustainability and showing that recycled materials can create beautiful, functional products.
