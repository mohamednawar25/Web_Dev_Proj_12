Our Recycling Process

Step 1: Collection

We gather plastic waste from local communities, schools, and businesses. Every piece of plastic collected is one less piece polluting our environment.

Step 2: Cleaning

All collected plastics are thoroughly cleaned and sorted by type. We remove any contaminants to ensure high-quality recycled material.

Step 3: Shredding

The clean plastic is shredded into small flakes. This increases the surface area and makes the material easier to process and mold.

Step 4: Molding

The plastic flakes are heated and molded into furniture components using specialized equipment and molds.

Step 5: Finishing

Components are assembled, polished, and painted. The result is beautiful, durable, eco-friendly furniture ready for your home!

Detailed Process Steps

  1. Collection: Gather plastic waste from local communities, schools, and recycling centers. We focus on clean, recyclable plastics like HDPE and PP.
  2. Cleaning: Remove impurities, labels, and contaminants. Plastics are washed and dried to prepare them for processing.
  3. Shredding: Break plastics into small reusable flakes using industrial shredders. The size depends on the final product requirements.
  4. Molding: Heat the plastic flakes to their melting point and inject them into custom-designed molds to create furniture components.
  5. Finishing: Assemble the components, sand rough edges, polish surfaces, and apply eco-friendly paints or finishes to create the final product.
